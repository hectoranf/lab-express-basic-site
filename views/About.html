Yayoi Kusama

“My life is a dot lost among thousands of other dots.”

Yayoi Kusama is a Japanese writer and contemporany artist. Her work is focused on sculpture and creating installations and performing. Kusama is one of the most popular avant-garde artists and one of the promoter of pop art.

Her work is based on conceptual art, by mixing surrealism, minimalism and pop art elements through patterns, colors and psychedelia.

She has been acknowledged as one of the most important living artists to come out of Japan.
